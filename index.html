Moonwake tracks your night with your iPhone and Apple Watch, listens for snoring on-device, and rings at the lightest moment of your morning — so waking up feels like surfacing, not sinking.

What it does

Smart wake

Your Apple Watch feels the moment you drift into light sleep near your alarm and wakes you there — gently, on the wrist.
