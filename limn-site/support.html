Frequently asked

How do I remove ads?

Open the in-game Store and choose “Remove Ads.” It's a one-time purchase that removes banner and full-screen ads.

I bought something but don't see it

Open the Store and tap “Restore Purchases.” Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the purchase.

How is my progress saved?

Your progress is stored locally on your device. Deleting the app removes it.
